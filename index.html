How it works
Set up in 60 seconds.
Connect your email, add subscriptions manually, or both. SubStack scans your billing emails and builds your dashboard automatically.
STEP 01
Connect your email
SubStack scans your inbox for billing confirmation emails and automatically detects your subscriptions. Takes 30 seconds.
STEP 02
Review your dashboard
See every subscription in one clean view — name, amount, next billing date, and category. Instantly know what you're paying for.
STEP 03
Set alerts & take action
Get notified before renewals. Flag subscriptions you want to review. Cancel what you don't need before you get charged.
